The **Industrial Data Platform** is designed to collect, process, and analyze sensor data from industrial environments, providing real-time reporting, email alerts, and a web-based dashboard for monitoring. It seamlessly integrates with **SCADA systems, mainframes, and other data sources**, ensuring compatibility with diverse industrial infrastructure. The platform supports multiple operational modes, including **interactive execution, batch processing for scheduled tasks, mainframe data imports, and continuous sensor monitoring**, making it adaptable to various industrial workflows.
