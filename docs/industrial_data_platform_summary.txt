The **Industrial Data Platform** is a Python-based system designed to collect, process, and analyze sensor data from industrial environments, providing real-time reporting, email alerts, and a web dashboard for monitoring. It integrates seamlessly with SCADA systems, mainframes, MQTT brokers, and serial devices, enabling automated data collection and batch processing. Targeted at developers, operators, and maintainers, the platform requires Python, pip, and access to MQTT brokers and serial ports.
