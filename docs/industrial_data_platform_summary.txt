# Industrial Data Platform (IDP) - Project Requirements Summary

The Industrial Data Platform (IDP) is a centralized system designed to **collect, process, and analyze industrial sensor data** from diverse sources, including **SCADA systems, mainframes, and IoT devices**. It provides **real-time monitoring, automated reporting, and email alerts** to enable data-driven decision-making, while supporting a **web-based dashboard** for visualization and operational insights. The platform integrates seamlessly with existing industrial infrastructure, ensuring scalability, security, and compliance with industry standards.
