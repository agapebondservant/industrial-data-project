The Industrial Data Platform (IDP) is a scalable solution designed to collect, process, and visualize real-time sensor and SCADA data for industrial operations. It integrates seamlessly with existing systems to enable automated reporting, email alerts for critical events, and a user-friendly web dashboard for monitoring key metrics. The platform supports API-driven access, ensuring flexibility for custom integrations and third-party tools. By centralizing data and providing actionable insights, IDP enhances operational efficiency and decision-making for stakeholders.
