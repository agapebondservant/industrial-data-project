The **Industrial Data Platform** is a Python-based system designed for **real-time sensor data collection, processing, and reporting**, with built-in support for **email alerts, web dashboards, and batch processing**. It integrates seamlessly with **SCADA systems, mainframes, MQTT brokers, and serial devices**, making it ideal for industrial environments requiring **automated monitoring and data aggregation**. Targeted at **developers, operators, and maintainers**, the platform relies on **Python, pip, MQTT, and serial communication protocols** for deployment. For detailed technical specifications, refer to the [codebase](https://github.com/rdwj/py2to3-example-project) or architecture diagrams.
